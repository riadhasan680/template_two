The Overwatch World Cup 2025 has been officially announced with the largest prize pool in the tournament's history and a revolutionary new format that promises to be the most exciting esports event of the year.

## Tournament Format

The 2025 World Cup features:
- 48 national teams (up from 32)
- Group stages in multiple regions
- Double elimination bracket
- Best-of-5 matches throughout
- Grand Finals as Best-of-7

## Prize Pool Distribution

The record-breaking $3 million prize pool:
- 1st place: $1,200,000
- 2nd place: $600,000
- 3rd place: $300,000
- 4th place: $150,000
- Remaining teams: $750,000 distributed

## New Features

World Cup 2025 introduces:
- Player cam with real-time heart rate monitoring
- Advanced statistics dashboard for viewers
- Interactive fan voting for MVP
- Regional qualifiers with live audience events
- Documentary series following teams

## Qualification Process

Teams can qualify through:
- Regional ranking points
- Online qualification tournaments
- Wild card selections by Blizzard
- Host country automatic qualification

The tournament will take place in August 2025, with finals in Los Angeles.

The beloved Zombies mode makes its triumphant return with the most ambitious story yet.

## New Story Arc

### Chapter 1: The Awakening
- **Setting**: Post-apocalyptic Tokyo 2045
- **Characters**: New cast with returning favorites
- **Villains**: Ancient evil awakened by scientific experiments

## Gameplay Innovations

### RPG Elements
- **Character Progression**: Level up individual abilities
- **Skill Trees**: Customizable power upgrades
- **Loot System**: Randomized weapon drops with rarities

### New Mechanics
- **Base Building**: Fortify locations between rounds
- **Vehicle Combat**: Driveable vehicles in zombie maps
- **Environmental Destruction**: Dynamic map changes

## Maps at Launch

- **Tokyo Rising**: Urban environment with verticality
- **Arctic Base**: Snow-based survival map
- **Desert Fortress**: Classic open area design

## Cross-Platform Support

Full cross-play and cross-progression across all platforms with dedicated servers.

Valorant players are celebrating the release of Haven Remastered, bringing new life to one of the game's most beloved maps. The update includes visual improvements and subtle gameplay adjustments.

## Visual Improvements

The remastered Haven features:
- Completely reworked textures and lighting
- Improved environmental details
- Better visibility for competitive play
- Updated sound design

## Gameplay Changes

Riot Games made several gameplay adjustments:
- Slightly resized some choke points
- Improved sightlines for defenders
- Added new cover positions
- Balanced attacker/defender advantages

## Competitive Impact

Professional players have been testing the new version, and early feedback suggests it will be well-received in the competitive scene.

The Haven Remastered is now available in all game modes, including competitive and tournament play.

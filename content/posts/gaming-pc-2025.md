Building a gaming PC in 2025 requires careful component selection for maximum performance and future-proofing.

## High-End Build ($3000+)

### CPU
- **Intel Core i9-14900K** or **AMD Ryzen 9 7950X3D**
- Best for gaming and content creation
- Excellent overclocking potential

### GPU
- **NVIDIA RTX 5080** (16GB VRAM)
- **AMD RX 7900 XTX** (24GB VRAM)
- Ray tracing performance leader

### Memory
- **32GB DDR5-6000** CL30
- Dual channel configuration
- RGB lighting options

### Storage
- **2TB NVMe Gen5 SSD** for OS and games
- **4TB SATA SSD** for additional storage

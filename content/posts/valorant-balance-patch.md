Valorant's latest balance patch has brought significant changes to multiple agents, completely reshaping the competitive meta. Patch 8.0 addresses several community concerns and introduces new strategic possibilities.

## Major Changes

The patch includes:
- Jett gets nerfed to her dash cooldown
- Sage receives buff to her healing ability
- Reyna gets ultimate cost reduction
- Killjoy utility costs adjusted
- New agent interactions added

## Community Reaction

The player community has mixed reactions to these changes. While many appreciate the Jett nerfs, others are concerned about how the Sage buff might affect defensive strategies.

## Professional Impact

Professional teams are already adapting their strategies to account for these changes. Several teams have announced they'll be experimenting with new agent compositions in upcoming tournaments.

Riot Games has stated they'll monitor the impact of these changes and make further adjustments as needed.

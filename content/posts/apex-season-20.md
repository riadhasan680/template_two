Apex Legends Season 20: Breakout is here with massive changes to the game.

## Legend Reworks

- **Pathfinder**: New passive ability
- **Wraith**: Portal distance increased
- **Gibraltar**: Shield health adjusted

## New Weapon

The Nemesis Burst AR joins the loot pool with unique charge-up mechanics.

## Map Updates

Kings Canyon receives its biggest overhaul since launch with new POIs and rotation paths.

PUBG Mobile is getting its biggest visual overhaul with the 3.0 update powered by Unreal Engine 5.

## Graphics Improvements

- **Ray Tracing**: Real-time reflections and shadows
- **120 FPS**: Support for high refresh rate displays
- **4K Textures**: Ultra-detailed environments

## New Features

The update also includes a new ranked mode and improved anti-cheat system.

## Device Requirements

Recommended: Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 or equivalent for maximum settings.

GTA 6 Online sets new standards for open-world multiplayer gaming with unprecedented features.

## Persistent World System

### Living City
- **24/7 NPC Schedules**: Realistic city life continues when offline
- **Player Housing**: Own and customize properties across the map
- **Business Empire**: Run legitimate and illegal enterprises
- **Stock Market**: Player-driven economy affects prices

### Multiplayer Innovations
- **200 Players**: Massive multiplayer sessions
- **Seamless Transitions**: No loading between activities
- **Cross-Platform**: Play with friends across all systems
- **Mobile App**: Manage empire from phone

## New Game Modes

### Heist 2.0
- **Complex Planning**: Multi-stage heists requiring coordination
- **Custom Approaches**: Multiple ways to complete objectives
- **AI Crew**: Hire NPC team members for solo players

### Empire Building
- **Criminal Organizations**: Create and manage gangs
- **Territory Wars**: Fight for control of neighborhoods
- **Legal Businesses**: Front companies for money laundering

## Graphics & Performance

- **Ray Tracing**: Full RT implementation on all platforms
- **60 FPS**: Stable performance with adaptive quality
- **4K Support**: Native resolution on next-gen consoles
- **VR Mode**: Optional virtual reality support

## Release Schedule

Beta testing begins Q2 2025 with full launch in Q4 2025.

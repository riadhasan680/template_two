Fortnite Chapter 5 brings the biggest changes to the game since its initial launch.

## New Island Features

- **Biome Diversity**: 5 distinct biomes with unique resources
- **Underground Areas**: New cave systems and hidden locations
- **Dynamic Weather**: Weather affects gameplay and visibility

## Building System Overhaul

### New Mechanics
- **Instant Build**: Faster structure placement
- **Material Types**: Wood, brick, metal with different strengths
- **Pre-built Structures**: Quick defensive options

### Zero Build Mode
- Enhanced movement abilities
- New tactical items
- Improved cover system

## Battle Pass Changes

- **100 Tiers**: More rewards than ever
- **Collaborations**: Major crossover events planned
- **Exclusive Skins**: Chapter 5 themed cosmetics

## Competitive Scene

New tournament format with $20 million prize pool for the first year.

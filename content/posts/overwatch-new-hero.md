Nexus arrives as the most complex support hero in Overwatch history with unique reality manipulation abilities.

## Hero Background

**Name**: Dr. Elena Vasquez  
**Age**: 34  
**Role**: Support  
**Difficulty**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

## Abilities Breakdown

### Primary Fire: Reality Beam
- **Healing**: 60 HP/s to allies
- **Damage**: 30 damage/s to enemies
- **Range**: 30 meters
- **Mechanic**: Switch between healing/damage modes

### Secondary Fire: Pocket Dimension
- **Effect**: Creates small portal that stores projectiles
- **Duration**: 3 seconds
- **Release**: All stored projectiles fire at once
- **Cooldown**: 12 seconds

### Passive: Quantum Sight
- See enemies through walls (limited range)
- Predict enemy ultimate charge
- Share vision with team

### Ultimate: Reality Shift
- **Effect**: Swap positions of any two players (allied or enemy)
- **Range**: Global
- **Duration**: Instant
- **Counterplay**: 0.5 second delay before swap

## Strategic Impact

Professional teams already developing new compositions around Nexus's unique abilities.

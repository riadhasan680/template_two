Fortnite's latest update has brought an exciting new mythic weapon that's completely changing how players approach the game. The Plasma Rifle has arrived with unique abilities that make it a must-have for competitive players.

## Weapon Features

The Plasma Rifle comes with several special abilities:
- Charged shots that deal extra damage
- Ability to see enemies through walls for 3 seconds
- Unlimited ammo but with overheating mechanic

## Community Response

Players have been experimenting with the new weapon, and many are calling it "game-changing." Professional players are already incorporating it into their tournament strategies.

## Balance Changes

Epic Games has stated they're monitoring the weapon's performance and may make adjustments in future updates to ensure balanced gameplay.

The Plasma Rifle is available now in all game modes and can be found in specific locations around the map.

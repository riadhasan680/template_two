Take your movement skills to the next level with these pro-level techniques used by ALGS players.

## Fundamental Movement

### Basic Strafing
- **ADAD**: Left-right movement while maintaining accuracy
- **Circle Strafe**: Move around opponents while keeping them centered
- **Jump Strafing**: Combine jumps with directional changes

### Slide Hopping
- **Timing**: Jump at the end of each slide
- **Direction**: Change direction between jumps
- **Momentum**: Maintain speed through proper timing

## Advanced Techniques

### Tap-Strafing (PC Only)
- **Input**: Quick tap of A or D while in air
- **Application**: Sharp direction changes mid-air
- **Limitations**: Controller players cannot perform

### Super-Gliding
- **Setup**: High ground launch with proper angle
- **Execution**: Hold crouch after peak height
- **Distance**: Covers massive map distances

### Wall Bouncing
- **Detection**: Identify bounceable surfaces
- **Timing**: Jump at contact point
- **Combination**: Chain multiple bounces

## Legend-Specific Movement

### Wraith
- **Portal Tricks**: Use portals for repositioning
- **Void Timing**: Maximize invulnerability frames
- **Combo**: Portal + phase for escapes

### Octane
- **Launch Pad**: Vertical and horizontal launches
- **Stim Timing**: Manage health effectively
- **Speed Combos**: Stim + slide hopping

### Pathfinder
- **Grapple Momentum**: Build speed through swings
- **90s**: Sharp angle turns while grappling
- **Re-grapple**: Quick successive grapples

### Valkyrie
- **Skydiving**: Control descent speed
- **Ultimate Positioning**: Strategic team relocations
- **Tactical Flight**: Quick vertical repositioning

Rockstar Games' GTA 6 has suffered another major leak, this time revealing extensive details about the Vice City setting and gameplay mechanics. The leak includes screenshots, gameplay footage, and internal documents.

## Vice City Setting Details

The leaked information shows:
- Modern-day Vice City with 1980s nostalgia elements
- Multiple explorable islands
- Dynamic weather system with hurricanes
- Fully functional underwater exploration
- Interactive nightlife venues

## Gameplay Features

New gameplay mechanics revealed:
- Advanced AI system for NPCs
- Multiple playable characters with story intertwining
- Deep customization options for vehicles and properties
- New business management systems
- Enhanced combat and driving mechanics

## Rockstar's Response

Rockstar Games has acknowledged the leak and urged fans to avoid spoilers. The company stated that the leaked content represents an early version of the game and many features may change before release.

Despite the leak, excitement for GTA 6 continues to build, with fans eagerly anticipating the official release.

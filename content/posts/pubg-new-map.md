Miramar gets a complete overhaul with new points of interest and improved gameplay flow.

## Major Changes

### New Locations
- **Neon City**: Urban area with tall buildings
- **Oasis Resort**: Luxury hotel complex
- **Military Base**: Expanded with underground tunnels
- **Research Facility**: Science-themed POI with unique loot

### Gameplay Improvements
- **Vehicle Spawns**: More varied and balanced
- **Loot Distribution**: Better tier spread across map
- **Cover System**: More natural terrain features
- **Blue Zone**: Adjusted timing for better pacing

## Visual Upgrades

- **Lighting**: Completely new day/night cycle
- **Weather**: Dynamic sandstorms and heat waves
- **Textures**: 4K resolution support
- **Draw Distance**: Improved visibility

## Tactical Changes

- **Long Range**: More sniper opportunities
- **Close Combat**: New CQC areas in cities
- **Vehicle Combat**: Better balance between vehicles and infantry
- **Third Person**: Improved camera angles

## Competitive Integration

New map immediately added to ranked rotation with professional tournaments planned.

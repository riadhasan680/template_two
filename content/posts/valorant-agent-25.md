Valorant's newest agent Chronomancer brings unprecedented control over time to the battlefield.

## Agent Profile

**Name**: Tempus  
**Role**: Controller  
**Origin**: Classified Omega Protocol experiment

## Abilities

### Signature Ability: Time Bubble
- Creates a sphere where time moves 50% slower for enemies
- Bullets and abilities are delayed inside the bubble
- Lasts for 4 seconds

### Basic Ability: Rewind
- Returns player to position from 3 seconds ago
- 45 second cooldown
- Can be used while falling or in air

### Ultimate: Chrono Shift
- Freezes all enemies in line of sight for 2 seconds
- Allies can move freely during freeze
- 7 ultimate points required

## Meta Impact

Professional teams already developing new strategies around time manipulation mechanics.

## Agent Synergies

- **Perfect with**: Jett, Reyna for aggressive plays
- **Counters**: Chamber, Killjoy for area denial
- **Weak against**: Sova, Cypher for information gathering

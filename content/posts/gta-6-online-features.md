Rockstar Games has officially revealed the first details about GTA 6 Online, promising a revolutionary multiplayer experience that builds on the success of GTA Online while introducing groundbreaking new features.

## Key Features

GTA 6 Online will include:
- Support for 200+ players in a single session
- Persistent world that evolves based on player actions
- Cross-platform play between all systems
- Player-driven economy with cryptocurrency integration
- Advanced anti-cheat system using machine learning

## New Game Modes

The online experience features:
- Massive heists requiring 50+ players
- Political system where players can run for office
- Business empire management with real-time stock market
- Custom content creation tools
- Esports tournaments with official prize pools

## Technical Innovations

Rockstar is introducing:
- Cloud-based streaming for seamless world loading
- AI-powered matchmaking system
- Real-time content updates without maintenance
- Advanced physics engine supporting complex interactions

## Beta Testing

Rockstar has announced that beta testing will begin in Q2 2025, with select players getting early access to provide feedback.

The gaming community is excited about these innovations, with many calling it "the future of online gaming."

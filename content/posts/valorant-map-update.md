Valorant introduces Abyss, the most vertically ambitious map in competitive FPS history.

## Map Overview

### Basic Information
- **Name**: Abyss
- **Location**: Underwater research facility
- **Theme**: Deep-sea exploration
- **Release**: Episode 9 Act 1

### Map Specifications
- **Size**: Large (similar to Haven)
- **Sites**: 2 spike plant sites
- **Levels**: 3 distinct vertical layers
- **Unique Feature**: Dynamic water mechanics

## Layout Design

### Three-Level Structure
- **Top Level**: Observatory and research areas
- **Mid Level**: Main facility and corridors
- **Bottom Level**: Underwater sections

### Site A - Research Lab
- **Entry Points**: 4 main approaches
- **Vertical Options**: 2 levels of height advantage
- **Cover Elements**: Lab equipment and glass walls
- **Retake Positions**: Multiple defensive angles

### Site B - Observatory
- **Open Design**: Large open area
- **Height Advantage**: Superior positions
- **Water Features**: Dynamic obstacles
- **Escape Routes**: Multiple exit paths

### Water System
- **Dynamic Water**: Rising and falling levels
- **Sound Dampening**: Muffled footsteps

- **Staircases**: Multiple connection points
- **Ziplines**: Quick vertical movement
- **Jump Pads**: Propulsion mechanics

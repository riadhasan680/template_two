Riot Games has officially revealed the newest agent coming to Valorant in Episode 8 Act 2.

## New Controller Agent

The agent, codenamed "Veil", brings a fresh take on the controller role with abilities that combine vision denial and area denial.

## Abilities Overview

- **Smoke Wall**: Creates a vertical wall of smoke
- **Toxic Cloud**: Damaging smoke that lingers
- **Ultimate**: Map-wide vision block

## Release Date

Expected to launch on December 20, 2025.

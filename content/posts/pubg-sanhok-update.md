PUBG Mobile's Sanhok map has received a comprehensive update that brings new locations and significant performance improvements. The update aims to refresh the popular map while maintaining its fast-paced gameplay.

## New Locations

The Sanhok update includes:
- Two new major points of interest
- Redesigned existing locations
- New road networks for better vehicle movement
- Additional cover and tactical positions

## Performance Improvements

Technical enhancements include:
- Better frame rates on mid-range devices
- Reduced loading times
- Improved texture quality
- Optimized network performance

## Community Response

Players have been exploring the updated map and sharing their experiences. The new locations have been particularly well-received by the competitive community.

The update is now available for all PUBG Mobile players, with Krafton promising continued support for the game.

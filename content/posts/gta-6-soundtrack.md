Rockstar Games has revealed details about the most ambitious music system ever created for a video game in GTA 6. The new system features dynamic radio stations, hundreds of licensed tracks, and innovative music integration.

## Radio Stations

GTA 6 will feature:
- 25+ radio stations with different genres
- Dynamic playlists that change based on time and location
- Celebrity DJs with exclusive content
- User-created custom stations
- Podcast and talk radio options

## Licensed Music

The soundtrack includes:
- 500+ licensed songs from major artists
- Exclusive tracks from popular musicians
- Classical and jazz collections
- International music from around the world
- Retro and modern hits spanning decades

## Dynamic Music System

Innovative features include:
- Context-aware music that adapts to gameplay
- Mood-based playlist generation
- Player preference learning
- Social music sharing with friends
- Live concert events in-game

## Technical Innovation

The music system uses:
- Advanced audio streaming technology
- Spatial audio for immersive experience
- Real-time music mixing
- AI-powered music recommendations
- Cross-platform music synchronization

Rockstar has promised regular music updates post-launch, keeping the soundtrack fresh and exciting.

Valorant introduces Agent 26 'Vex', a mysterious shadow manipulator from the Void.

## Agent Profile

### Background
- **Name**: Vex (Real name: Elena Volkov)
- **Origin**: Moscow, Russia
- **Age**: 28
- **Affiliation**: Former Kingdom operative
- **Role**: Initiator

### Abilities Overview
- **Q**: Shadow Step (Mobility)
- **E**: Void Zone (Area Denial)
- **C**: Dark Pulse (Information)
- **X**: Shadow Realm (Ultimate)

## Ability Breakdown

### Shadow Step (Q)
- **Cost**: 200 credits
- **Charges**: 2
- **Duration**: 3 seconds
- **Range**: 25 meters

**Mechanics**:
- Transform into shadow for brief duration
- Pass through enemies and walls
- Leave trail that damages enemies
- Can be canceled early

### Void Zone (E)
- **Cost**: 300 credits
- **Duration**: 8 seconds
- **Radius**: 15 meters
- **Effects**: Slow + Decay

**Mechanics**:
- Create area of shadow energy
- Enemies inside take decay damage
- Movement speed reduced by 50%
- Blocks enemy abilities

### Dark Pulse (C)
- **Cost**: Free (1 charge per round)
- **Duration**: 4 seconds
- **Range**: 30 meters
- **Cooldown**: 35 seconds

**Mechanics**:
- Send pulse of shadow energy
- Reveals enemy positions
- Disrupts enemy abilities
- Provides team intel

### Shadow Realm (X)
- **Cost**: 7 ultimate points
- **Duration**: 12 seconds
- **Radius**: 20 meters
- **Effects**: Global presence

**Mechanics**:
- Enter shadow realm state
- See through walls globally
- Teleport to any shadow location
- Team gains shadow vision

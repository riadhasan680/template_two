Season 21 brings revolutionary changes to Apex Legends with new content and gameplay improvements.

## New Legend: Vortex

### Abilities
- **Passive**: Spatial Awareness - See enemies through walls for 2 seconds after taking damage
- **Tactical**: Gravity Well - Pull enemies toward a central point
- **Ultimate**: Dimensional Rift - Create two portals for team movement

### Playstyle
Vortex excels at crowd control and team repositioning, making him perfect for coordinated teams.

## Battle Pass Highlights

### Free Tier Rewards
- New Vortex skin at Tier 25
- Weapon charm collection
- Apex Packs at Tiers 15, 30, 45

### Premium Tier Rewards
- Legendary Vortex heirloom at Tier 100
- 24 new weapon skins
- Exclusive loading screens and banners

## Major Gameplay Changes

### Weapon Meta Shifts
- **R-301**: Slight damage reduction
- **Wingman**: Increased hip fire spread
- **Peacekeeper**: Faster reload speed
- **Charge Rifle**: Reduced charge time

### Map Rotations
- **World's Edge**: Permanent addition with new POIs
- **Storm Point**: Removed for rework
- **Olympus**: Returns with updated lighting

### Quality of Life Updates
- Improved ping system
- Better loot distribution
- Enhanced audio cues
- Faster matchmaking

## Ranked Changes

### New Ranking System
- Demotion protection added
- Better RP gains for high placements
- Reduced entry costs for higher ranks

### Seasonal Rewards
- Exclusive dive trails for Masters/Predators
- New badge system
- Account-wide rank tracking

## Esports Integration

### ALGS Updates
- New tournament format
- Increased prize pool
- Regional qualifiers revamped

### Pro League Features
- Weekly tournaments
- Viewer voting systems
- Enhanced spectator mode

Season 21 represents the biggest update in Apex Legends history, setting the stage for an exciting competitive year.

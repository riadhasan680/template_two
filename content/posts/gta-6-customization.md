Rockstar Games has unveiled the most advanced character customization system ever seen in a video game for GTA 6. The new system allows players to create truly unique characters with unprecedented detail.

## Physical Customization

Players can customize:
- Full body morphing with hundreds of sliders
- Realistic aging and weathering effects
- Dynamic hair growth system
- Tattoo placement with custom designs
- Scars and injury history

## Clothing & Style

The clothing system includes:
- Thousands of clothing items from real brands
- Layer system for complex outfits
- Custom tailoring for perfect fit
- Seasonal collections that update regularly
- Designer collaborations

## Lifestyle Customization

Character lifestyle options:
- Voice customization with accent options
- Walking animations and mannerisms
- Skill development affecting gameplay
- Relationship building with NPCs
- Personal living spaces with decoration

## Technical Features

The customization system uses:
- AI-powered suggestion engine
- Photorealistic rendering
- Real-time physics for clothing
- Persistent customization across all game modes

Rockstar has stated this is just the beginning, with more customization options planned for post-launch updates.

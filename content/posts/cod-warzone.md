Activision has launched Urzikstan, the biggest Call of Duty Warzone map to date.

## Map Features

- **Size**: 3x larger than Verdansk
- **POIs**: Over 300 unique locations
- **Terrain**: Desert, urban, and mountain regions

## New Mechanics

The map introduces dynamic weather and destructible buildings.

## Player Reception

Early feedback has been overwhelmingly positive with players praising the variety.

The Fortnite World Cup returns in 2025 with the largest prize pool in battle royale history.

## Tournament Structure

- **Qualifiers**: Online tournaments across all regions
- **Finals**: LAN event in Los Angeles
- **Format**: Solo and Duo competitions

## Prize Distribution

- **1st Place**: $3 Million
- **2nd Place**: $2 Million  
- **3rd Place**: $1 Million
- **Remaining**: $4 Million distributed among top 100

## New Features

Tournament includes creative mode competitions and a celebrity pro-am event.

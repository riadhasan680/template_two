Master the competitive ladder in Apex Legends Mobile with our comprehensive ranked guide.

## Rank Tiers Explained

### Bronze to Silver
- Focus on basic mechanics and legend abilities
- Learn popular drop locations and loot routes
- Practice aim training daily

### Gold to Platinum
- Master advanced movement techniques
- Coordinate with teammates effectively
- Understand current meta legends

### Diamond and Above
- Perfect your game sense and positioning
- Learn pro-level strats and rotations
- Maintain consistent performance

## Best Legends for Ranked

### S-Tier
- **Wraith**: Essential for safe rotations
- **Gibraltar**: Defensive powerhouse
- **Bloodhound**: Ultimate recon advantage

### A-Tier
- **Lifeline**: Team sustain
- **Pathfinder**: Vertical mobility
- **Octane**: Aggressive repositioning

## Pro Tips

1. **Hot Drop Mastery**: Learn to win early fights
2. **Loot Efficiency**: Optimize your inventory management
3. **Team Communication**: Use pings and voice effectively
4. **Zone Control**: Always know the next circle position
5. **Survival First**: Sometimes running is the best play

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Overextending without teammates
- Poor inventory management
- Ignoring zone timing
- Fighting without purpose
- Not using cover effectively

Master these fundamentals and watch your rank climb steadily.

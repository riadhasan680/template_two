The PUBG Global Championship 2025 has been officially announced with the largest prize pool in the game's history. Krafton is promising an unprecedented esports spectacle for fans worldwide.

## Tournament Details

The championship features:
- $5 million prize pool (largest ever)
- 32 teams from around the world
- Three-week tournament format
- Multiple game modes including classic and custom matches

## Qualification Process

Teams can qualify through:
- Regional leagues (Asia, Europe, Americas)
- Online qualifiers
- Performance-based invitations
- Last chance qualifiers

## New Features

PGC 2025 introduces:
- Enhanced spectator experience
- Player cam integration
- Real-time statistics display
- Interactive viewer features

## Expected Impact

The esports community is excited about this announcement, with many calling it a "game-changer" for competitive PUBG. Several organizations have already announced increased investment in their PUBG rosters.

The tournament is scheduled for November 2025, with qualifiers starting in June.

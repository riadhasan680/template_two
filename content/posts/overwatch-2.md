Overwatch 2 Season 8 introduces a brand new support hero with game-changing abilities.

## Hero Overview

The new hero, named "Echo", specializes in burst healing and damage amplification.

## Abilities

- **Healing Burst**: AOE heal with damage boost
- **Shield Generator**: Temporary shields for allies
- **Ultimate**: Team-wide resurrection

## Competitive Impact

Pro players are already theorycrafting new team compositions around this hero.
